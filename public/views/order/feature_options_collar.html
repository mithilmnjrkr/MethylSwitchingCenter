Semi Spread

The semi-spread collar is a well-balanced modern collar that works well for both casual and dress occasions.

Full Spread

The full spread collar is unquestionably formal and elegant. Ideal for a wider tie know (think windsor or half-windsor).

Point Spread

The point collar is our narrowest spread. It works great with a skinny tie, or if you want the collar points to sit closer to your neck when open.

Double Button

The double button collar is a bit larger and more robust.

Button Down

The Sartor button-down is a fresh take on an American classic. It has a medium spread and shorter collar flaps for a modern, casual look.
